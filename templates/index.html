Welcome to Anatomy

Anatomy is a frontend application to help consumers view exported chats in a familiar user-interface.
The tool is for quick previewing of chats; chat exports viewed on Anatomy are deleted after parsing and cannot be viewed again.
Consider using aknClient or Aria for long-term communications storage
You can upload your export in .txt or .zip form.
